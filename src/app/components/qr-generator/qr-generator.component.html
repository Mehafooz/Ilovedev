About QR Codes

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of information. They're widely used for quick access to websites, product information, payment systems, and more.

📱 Common Uses

  • Website URLs and landing pages
  • WiFi network credentials
  • Contact information (vCard)
  • Payment links and crypto wallets
  • Product packaging and menus
  • Event tickets and check-ins
  • App download links

🎯 Best Practices

  • Keep URLs short for smaller codes
  • Test codes before printing
  • Ensure high contrast colors
  • Leave white space around code
  • Use medium error correction
  • Minimum size: 2x2 cm for print

🔧 Error Correction Levels

  • Low (7%): Fastest scanning
  • Medium (15%): Balanced choice
  • Quartile (25%): Good durability
  • High (30%): Maximum recovery

Higher levels = larger codes but better damage resistance

💡 QR Code Formats

  • URL: https://example.com
  • Email: mailto:email@example.com
  • Phone: tel:+1234567890
  • SMS: sms:+1234567890
  • WiFi: WIFI:T:WPA;S:name;P:pass;;
  • GPS: geo:lat,lng
